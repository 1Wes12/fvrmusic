Wesley Russell, FVR Biography

I am a 17 year old kid that loves music. I have a big passion for music because it helps ease my mind.
When I am going through tough times and I can't slow down the thoughts in my head, I write it down
and make a song about it. I have gone through a lot lately and I think music is one of the main reasons
of why I am still moving forward. When I feel a certain way I turn on a song that I made about that
certain feeling, If I feel something I haven't felt before, I'll make a song about it to listen to. I don't just
make them for the heck of it, I make it to help me and the other people get through this and know that
they aren't alone in this cruel world. We all go through tough times, but we also are all strong enough
to work through it and better yourself and the others around you.

Music Journey

I started making music in 2024, it was one of the best decisions I have made. I love making music about
my life including the problems I face, the mistakes I have made. I also enjoy making up stories to write
about that gets you in the moment and some people could possibly/hopefully relate to. It is just a very
big passion of mine and I would like to continue making songs no matter how big I am, it helps me and the
others that go through what I do. It's like a coping mechanism.

Why Do I Use AI and How

I use Suno to create my music. I write all my lyrics in google docs and type out what genre, voices, and
instruments I would prefer in my music with the different verses. So it is still all original and all made by
me. I use AI because I don't have a good voice. I can write good lyrics but I can't sing well. So I used what
I'm good with, computers. I create and program my songs to be exactly how I want and need them to be.
